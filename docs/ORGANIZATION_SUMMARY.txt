🚀 QUICK START:

1. Setup:
   chmod +x setup.sh && ./setup.sh

2. Configure:
   cp src/conf.py.example src/conf.py
   nano src/conf.py  # Edit with your credentials

3. Add Resume:
   cp ~/path/to/Resume.pdf data/

4. Run:
   source venv/bin/activate
   python3 src/bot.py
